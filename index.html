From Central Plant to VRF/VRV & DX System Design, I've delivered turnkey HVAC solutions for hospitals, high-rise offices and luxury residences — always on budget, always on spec.

What I Do

Core
Services

End-to-end HVAC Design Support —
from feasibility study to final commissioning and ongoing Site Execution.

01

System Design & Engineering

Load calculations, duct sizing, equipment selection, and full HVAC system design using industry-standard software including Carrier HAP, Trane Psychrometic Calculator, Autodesk AutoCAD and Autodesk Revit.

02

Technical Specifications & Tender Documents

Submission of Technical details of equipments for the proposed design along with actual Material quantity and Estimate Cost of the product/project.

03

Energy Auditing & Optimization

Comprehensive energy assessments, ASHRAE Level I–III audits, and retrofit planning that consistently delivers 20–40% reduction in HVAC energy consumption.

04

BMS / Smart Controls

Coordination and Support with Building Management Systems (BMS) Design & Execution Team for Intigration of BMS, DDC controls, and IoT-based monitoring platforms — Siemens / Honeywell and open-protocol
BACnet / Modbus.

05

Air Quality & Ventilation

IAQ assessments, HEPA and UV-C filtration upgrades, ERV/HRV installations, and ventilation design per ASHRAE 62.1 for healthy, compliant indoor environments.
